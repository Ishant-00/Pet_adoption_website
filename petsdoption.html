Meet Husky

Siberian Husky, a thickly coated, compact sled dog of medium size and great endurance, was developed to work in packs, pulling light loads at moderate speeds over vast frozen expanses. Sibes are friendly, fastidious, and dignified. The graceful, medium-sized Siberian Husky's almond-shaped eyes can be either brown or blue'and sometimes one of each.

Meet Jack

Spitz dogs are known for their curly tails, and the Japanese spitz is no exception. This medium-sized fluffy pup has distinctive foxlike facial features, and they boast a happy, outgoing personality that many people love. The white fur of this pup is often quite fluffy, and they need regular brushing to prevent tangles.The Japanese Spitz, as a companion breed, enjoy the company of their families and truly are little comedians. They love to please their family and are thus eager to learn; they always have a smile on their face.

Meet Bella

Bella is a charming tabby cat with soft, mottled fur that glistens in the sunlight. Her emerald-green eyes sparkle with curiosity and mischief, reflecting her playful spirit. Bella loves to lounge in sunny spots, often stretching out luxuriously, but she’s always ready to pounce on a passing toy or investigate a rustling sound. She has a sweet, melodic purr that resonates whenever she’s being petted, and she enjoys snuggling up on laps during cozy evenings. Bella is also quite the explorer, often found perched on windowsills, watching birds flit by or observing the world outside. With her playful antics and affectionate nature, to her home.

Meet Niko

Niko is a sleek black cat with striking yellow eyes that seem to glow in the dark. His coat is glossy and well-groomed, and he moves with a graceful, almost regal demeanor. Known for his playful personality, Niko loves to chase after feather toys and pounce on anything that moves, displaying a burst of energy that keeps everyone entertained. When he’s not playing, you can find him perched on a high shelf, surveying his domain with a confident air. Niko has a gentle side, too; he enjoys curling up next to his favorite human, often letting out a soft, soothing purr that fills the room. With his curious nature.

Meet Riko

Riko is a fluffy calico cat with a patchwork of orange, black, and white fur that gives her a unique and vibrant appearance. Her big, bright blue eyes are full of curiosity, and she has a playful yet gentle personality. Riko loves to explore her surroundings, often investigating every nook and cranny with a mix of caution and excitement. When she’s not on an adventure, Riko enjoys lounging in cozy spots, especially sunbeams streaming through the window. She has a knack for finding the comfiest places to nap, curling up like a little ball of warmth. Riko is also quite the cuddler; she loves to snuggle up with her favorite humans.
